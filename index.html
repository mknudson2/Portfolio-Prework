Researcher

I have spent the last 15 years in academia not only pursuing my own education, but working to further my areas of specialization through scholarly research, translation, and—perhaps my favorite—in the classroom, whether virtual or in-person.

My specialities lie in Viking and Medieval Scandinavian studies, Old Norse philology, medieval religious studies (Christian and pagan), as well as areas of Anglo-Saxon and Celtic languages, literatures, and cultures.

I eagerly look for opportunities to not only share my knowledge, but to find new ways to make it more accessible and approachable.
